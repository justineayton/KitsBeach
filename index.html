SWIMMING POOL

Kitsilano Pool is Vancouver's only saltwater swimming pool. The outdoor summer pool is located near the beach, cafés, and neighbourhood of Kitsilano.
Children under 8 must be with a responsible guardian (16+ years old)
Length: 137 metres
Features: Salt water pool, 3 Slides, Café
Accessibility: Wheelchair accessible, Aquatic wheelchairs, Aquatic pool lift
Passes accepted: Flexipass, Leisure Access Card (LAC)

PLAYGROUND

A playground is the park's newest feature. The city's largest fully accessible playground is a legacy of the Vancouver 2010 Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games. New play opportunities such as sand play table, rotating climber, and saucer swings have been included to ensure universal access and encourage imaginative play, sensory experiences and fun for children of all ages and abilities. A wheelchair accessible surface also allows parents and caregivers with physical disabilities access to the playground.

WATERPARK

VOLLEYBALL

Kitsilano Beach is equipt with 7 permanent sand volleyball courts. They are officially open from 11:30 AM to 8:30 PM. Play areas are available on a first come, first served basis. Courts don't require a reservation unless you are organizing a league practice or tournament. There is no time limit for volleyball court use.

TENNIS

Casual tennis play operates on a first-come, first-served basis under the following rules: 2 - 4 players shall wait facing a court to claim it 30 minutes maximum play if players are waiting rubber soled shoes must be worn players must avoid interference with play on adjoining courts tennis club may exceed 30 minutes playing time on permitted courts during club and league play.

BASKETBALL

Kit’s Beach has 2 outdoor basketball courts that are used on a first come, first serve basis. Bring a ball and some friends and enjoy playing in the beautiful scenery that is Kitsilano Beach.
